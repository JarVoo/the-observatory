# The Observatory

A data initiative monitoring tourism, visitor impact, and economic
trends across Gibraltar and the Campo de Gibraltar region.

## Status
Active — Gibraltar tourism analysis complete. Campo de Gibraltar 
data collection in progress.

## Current Analysis
### Gibraltar Tourism (1970-2023)
54 years of visitor data across air, sea, and land entry points.

Key findings:
- Land frontier accounts for over 90% of all visitor arrivals
- Peak year was 2011 with 11.9 million visitors
- COVID caused a 52% decline in 2020
- 2023 recovery stands at 82% of pre-COVID levels

## Focus Areas
- Tourism & visitor numbers
- Cross-border movement
- Economic impact of tourism

## Geographic Scope
